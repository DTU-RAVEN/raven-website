# Welcome to RAVEN DTU

We're excited to announce the official launch of RAVEN - Robotics, Autonomous Vehicle Engineering & Navigation - a new student organization at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU).

## Our Mission

RAVEN aims to provide a collaborative environment for students interested in robotics, autonomous systems, and navigation technologies. Whether you're studying electrical engineering, computer science, mechanical engineering, or any related field, there's a place for you in our organization.

## Get Involved

We're currently recruiting members for various teams:

- **Software Development**: Working on perception, control algorithms, and simulation
- **Mechanical Design**: Building the physical structure of our robots and vehicles
- **Electronics**: Developing sensor interfaces, power systems, and control boards
- **Project Management**: Coordinating our efforts and planning our participation in competitions

## Upcoming Events

Stay tuned for our kickoff meeting where we'll introduce the leadership team, discuss our first project, and answer any questions you might have about joining.

Looking forward to building amazing things together!
